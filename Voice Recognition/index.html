Instructions
  • Press Speak to begin speech recognition and Stop to end speech recognition.
  • Say "color _____" to change the color of the circle.
  • Say "background _____" to change the background color of the canvas.
  • Say "size _____" to change the size of the circle.
  • Say "help" to get help.
  • Say "about" to learn about the program.
  • If an incorrect size is detected, you will be notified.
